Frequently Asked Questions

How does our product work?

Hive leverages Django and Firebase to provide a web platform through users can submit data to users with open projects. This data is loaded onto an easily accessible databse powered by Dropbase, in the format of CSV files.

Who owns the data that is collected?

Your database and all data within it belongs to you, other users cannot view your database, only make file submissions to it.
